Cherry Cheesecake Cupcakes


So tasty! These cherry cheesecake cupcakes are sure to disappear quickly after you make them. Good recipe to make the day before serving.

Ingredients

Crust:

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • ¾ cup butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

Filling:

  • 2 (8 ounce) packages whipped cream cheese
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Topping:

  • 1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line 24 muffin cups with foil liners.

  2. Step 2

    3. Make the crust: Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar together in a bowl. Press crumbs into the bottoms of the prepared muffin cups.

  3. Step 3

    4. Make the filling: Beat cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and vanilla in a bowl until smooth. Divide filling between muffin cups.

  4. Step 4

    5. Bake in the preheated oven until cupcake tops are golden around the edges and slightly cracked, about 10 minutes.

  5. Step 5

    6. Remove from the oven and let cool completely, about 30 minutes.

  6. Step 6

    7. Top cooled cupcakes with cherry pie filling.
